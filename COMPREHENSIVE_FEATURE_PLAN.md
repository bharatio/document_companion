## 📄 Document Types Supported

### 1. Scanned Documents
- **Receipts** (grocery, restaurant, online purchases)
- **Invoices** (utilities, services, subscriptions)
- **Contracts** (rental, employment, service agreements)
- **Forms** (government, applications, surveys)
- **Legal Documents** (wills, deeds, certificates)
- **Medical Records** (prescriptions, test results, insurance cards)
- **Educational Documents** (diplomas, transcripts, certificates)
- **Financial Documents** (bank statements, tax forms, pay stubs)
- **ID Documents** (passport, driver's license, ID cards)
- **Business Cards**
- **Handwritten Notes** (meeting notes, sketches, reminders)
- **Whiteboard/Blackboard** captures
- **Magazine/Newspaper** articles
- **Books/Manuals** pages

### 2. Digital Documents
- **PDFs** (all formats and versions)
- **Images** (JPEG, PNG, TIFF, HEIC, WebP)
- **Office Documents** (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
- **Text Files** (TXT, RTF, Markdown)
- **Spreadsheets** (CSV, Excel)
- **Presentations** (PowerPoint, Keynote)

### 3. Specialized Documents
- **Multi-page Documents** (reports, books, manuals)
- **Signed Documents** (contracts with signatures)
- **Annotated Documents** (with highlights, notes, drawings)
- **Archived Documents** (old documents, historical records)
- **Compressed Documents** (ZIP files containing documents)
